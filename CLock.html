Assignment

The assignment was to program a clock, which can show the different parts of the day. It also had to include an animation. This assignment left a lot of room for creativity and freedom. I had two weeks to make it.

Process

I got inspired by different apps where the background changes based on the weather. Instead of using the weather, I took time as an inspiration.
