Illustrations

Assignment

Throughout the years I’ve made a lot of illustrations with Adobe Illustrator. These illustrations I’ve made during my free time and during projects from university and internship. They are made to either visually support the digital product or making it more pleasing to the eye.

Process

My process of these illustrations differ from project to project, but my general process goes like the following:

1. I start with sketching on paper. I make thumbnails to generate ideas because the first idea isn’t always the best idea and I want to explore what different opportunities my mind may come up with.
2. After I carefully judged all the ideas I put on paper, I discuss the most appealing ideas with the client before I continue working the illustration out.
3. When I start working the Illustration out, I make multiple versions color or shape-wise to explore different ways of how to work this particular illustration out.
4. After multiple iterations, and discussions with the client, the illustration is done and I will neatly deliver the illustration in the wished format.
